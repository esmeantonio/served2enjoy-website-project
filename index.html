About Us:

Our founder and chef, Luis Sanchez, was born and raised in San Bartolom√© Quialana, Oaxaca. It was here that he began to experiment with food at an early age. Luis vividly recalls the joy, happiness, and unity that cooking brought to him and the community. People would gather to cook for special events; the women would fire up grills, griddles, and pots to make fresh tortillas, moles, and a variety of other dishes. They were prepared with the utmost attention and love as well as with fresh ingredients which were grown locally. The Central Valley region of Oaxaca provided the community with the opportunity to grow their own vegetables and tropical fruits. The ingredients were also traded at times at the weekly Mercado.


Luis later moved to Los Angeles, in search of opportunities and worked his way up to kitchen manager at Koo Koo Roo, Santa Monica in the early 2000's. He later became the head chef for their Healthy Lunch Program. For the past two years, Luis has expanded his business alongside his wife, Ariana, and together they have opened their first sit-in location in South LA. He continues to incorporate his upbringing and love for fresh and healthy ingredients into the meals prepared here at Served2Enjoy. Our goal is to create and share those feelings of happiness and unity through our meals with the community.
